Update Address and Profile Details

Eligibility Requirements

Select "Individual" for your own profile management transaction or "Business" if the transaction is for a business. You cannotplace both an "individual" and a "business" customer transaction in the same shopping cart. To change the customer type, youwill need to sign out and sign in again.

Select Customer Type

  • You will need the following to modify your profile information online:
  • The last four digits of your Social Security number
  • Your Michigan driver's license or state ID card number
  • Your date of birth and eye color

Please Note: Changes of address affect all Secretary of State files associated with your name. For instance, this includes your driver's license or state ID card, vehicle records and voter registration. A change of address also applies to all transactions in your shopping cart.

A change of address cannot be completed online if you have:

  • Ordered a duplicate of a driver's license, state ID card, title or disability parking placard the same day
  • Submitted a change of address within the last 10 days
  • You will need the following to modify your profile information online:
  • The name of your business as it is on file with the Department of State
  • The business ZIP code
  • The title number from a vehicle with a Michigan title issued in the business's name
  • The last four digits of the Social Security number, driver's license or state ID card number, date of birth and eye color of the employee requesting to update the customer profile information

Online change of address is not yet available for businesses, although we hope to offer this service soon. Please visit a Secretary of State office to change your business address. Businesses may update their contact information and e-mail addresses through this online transaction.


Note: Record Keeping: A receipt will be generated for your records at the end of this transaction. If you do not have a printer, save a copy of the receipt to your files.

To ensure your printer is working properly, click the Test Printer button now.
